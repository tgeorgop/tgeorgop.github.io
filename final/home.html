Create Interactive Content with H5P

Are you looking for ways to motivate your students? H5P is a technology that gives instructors access to a large toolkit of applications to use in creating engaging, interactive instructional experiences within Canvas.

This site was created to provide you with an overview of H5P, the benefits and strategies for using H5P in the classroom, examples of H5P activities, and a guide for creating an H5P assignment in Canvas.
